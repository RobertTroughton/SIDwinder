# SIDquake robots policy

# The site itself is fine to index. But the raw HVSC mirror is NOT for
# crawling: search results should lead people to SIDquake pages, not to raw
# .sid download links. The whole collection is a free download at
# https://hvsc.c64.org/, so there's no need for crawlers to pull it from us.
