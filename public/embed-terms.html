HVSC Embed — Terms of Use

SIDquake hosts a mirror of the High Voltage SID Collection (HVSC) and offers an embeddable browser so other sites can let visitors find and preview C64 SID music. These terms cover using that embed.

The music: copyright & HVSC

HVSC is a free-ware collection, curated by the volunteer HVSC Crew — but the tunes themselves are not public domain. Per the HVSC documentation:

  • All SID tunes are copyright of their original composers (or the relevant software house / publisher).
  • They are provided for private listening enjoyment. Any other use — including commercial use, or use on YouTube and similar — requires permission from the rights holder.
  • The tunes are not suitable for release under Creative Commons or similar licences without a separate agreement with the rights holder.
  • Neither SIDquake nor the HVSC Crew can grant music licences or collect royalties. For permissions, contact the original composer; a useful first point of contact is chris@c64audio.com.

Full terms ship with the collection in HVSC.txt and STIL.txt.

Using the SIDquake embed

You're welcome to embed the SIDquake HVSC browser on your own site, provided you:

  • Keep the attribution intact — the "Powered by SIDquake" credit and the HVSC link shown in the embed must remain visible and unobstructed.
  • Embed it as provided (the iframe widget). Please don't scrape, bulk-download, or re-host the SID files or the index — the whole collection is a free download directly from hvsc.c64.org, so there's no need to pull it from us.
  • Respect visitors' use — the embed is for private listening and discovery, subject to the music terms above.
